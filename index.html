Dr. Han Wang

Dr. Han Wang will join the Department of Electrical Engineering & Computer Science at University of Kansas as an Assistant Professor in the Fall 2023. Han Wang received the Ph.D degree from the Department of Computer Science at Illinois Institute of Technology, advised by Dr. Yuan Hong. She got her M.Sc. degree in Software Engineering from Huazhong University of Science and Technology, China. Her research focuses on security and privacy (e.g., differential privacy, local differential privacy, secure multiparty computation, and applied cryptography), as well as trustworthy machine learning (e.g., attack, robustness, privacy, and fairness). She has published numerous research results in top security and data science conferences and journals, such as S&P/Oakland, CCS, KDD, PETS, ICDE, EDBT, ICDCS, TDSC and TKDE.
